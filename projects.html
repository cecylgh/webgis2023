StoryMaps

"Jejak Budaya Situs Purbakala"

The Archaeological Park Museum site located in Cipari Village, Cigugur District, Kuningan Regency is a valuable cultural heritage that is often not understood by the public. Through observations on the Site as well as interactions with guards and educators at the museum, it was revealed that visitor visits tend to occur infrequently. This indicates the need for a more attractive approach to invite public interest in understanding and appreciating this cultural wealth, even to the point of visiting the site in person. Therefore, I created StoryMaps that combine narration, interesting photos, short videos, and information about Archaeological Sites, to make it easier for people to understand and appreciate this cultural heritage. This approach is expected to attract the attention of the public and encourage them to visit the Site and take part in this interesting and valuable cultural journey.

StoryMaps

Location : Kuningan, West Java, Indonesia

Date : June 2023

Project Type : Geospatial
